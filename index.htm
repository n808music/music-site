N8:08

Long Nite, the first single off of N8:08's debut solo project Look What You've Done, marked his triumphant return to the rap game pairing aggressive flows with booming bass.

Look What You've Done was N8:08's debut solo project. Premiered on Fake Shore Drive, the project has earned hundreds of thousands of spins across platforms around the world. Titled as a response to the birth of his daughter, N8:08 captures the hearts of fans with many styles and flows.

Texas was the second single off of Look What You've Done. After premiering on the midwest's leading hip hop authority, Fake Shore Drive, Texas earned the support of many fans for its raw storytelling.
